Safety Tips

Meet in Public

Always meet in a public place for your first few dates. Choose well-lit, populated areas.

Tell Someone

Let a friend or family member know where you're going and who you're meeting.

Arrange Your Own Transport

Don't rely on your date for transportation, especially on early dates.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels off, it probably is. It's okay to end the date early.

Report Suspicious Behavior

Use the report feature if someone makes you feel unsafe or uncomfortable.
