How Heartly works

Heartly helps users create profiles, discover people, chat, post on the feed, and use the AI coach for safer, better conversations.

1 Complete your profile.
2 Discover people and view profiles.
3 Start respectful conversations.
4 Use Heartly AI for message and safety support.
Safety guidance

Keep your personal information private and stay alert when meeting or chatting with new people.

  • Do not share passwords, private codes, or financial details.
  • Do not send money to people you meet online.
  • Meet in public places if you decide to meet someone.
  • Report suspicious, rude, or unsafe behavior.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I edit my profile?
Go to Profile, then tap Edit Profile. You can update your photo, bio, location, interests, and preferences.
Can I change my username?
Yes. Go to Profile, open Account Settings, then choose a new username. Usernames must be unique.
What does Heartly AI do?
Heartly AI helps with respectful message ideas, profile improvements, conversation advice, and safety guidance.
What happens when I block someone?
Blocked users should not be able to contact you. The blocked users list will show people you have blocked.

Stay safe on Heartly

If someone makes you uncomfortable, stop replying, block them, and report the issue. Your safety comes first.
